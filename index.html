Our Boxes


The boxes consist of a mixture of Brownies, Blondies and Brookies that have been combined with your ultimate favourite chocolate bars, biccies and sweet treats to create the most insane flavours and sensational textures that will blow your mind.

Each week, you will find different combinations to keep those taste buds loving life.

Delivery and Collection Info


All boxes are sent nationwide via Royal Mail First Class. Please allow 1-4 working days for delivery from our dispatch day (Tuesdays). Last orders for the next dispatch day are 12PM on Saturday.

If you wish to collect, please email hello@beautbakes.co.uk or use the contact form to arrange a day and time.
